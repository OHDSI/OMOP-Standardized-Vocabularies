Release Notes 7-Aug-2017

Fixed

1. RxNorm Extension. Brand Names, Ingredients, Suppliers and corresponding drugs are cleaned up.

2. ATC. Multicomponent drugs that have link to ATC doesn't have this ATC related to their Ingredients.

3. dm+d. Multiple mappings removed. dm+d - RxNorm Extension relationships are built according to the new RxNorm Extension requirements.

Added

1. CVX vocabulary. Has relationships to RxNorm and RxNorm Extension and ATC-like hierarchy.

Updated

1. Gemscript. Added mappings that weren't covered with Gemscript - dm+d mapping.

2. Read. Read vaccines concepts now have mappings to CVX or RxNorm.
